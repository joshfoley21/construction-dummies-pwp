Construction Dummies Purpose, Audience, and Goals


Purpose

The purpose of this website is to create a functioning website for my friends construction business. I would like to connect the audience to a local service with local people.

Construction Dummies is a business that specializes in commercial/residential clean outs, demolition, junk haul, and new construction clean up. This company is still fairly new and only operates from small social media pages and word of mouth.

Once the website is created the goal is to bring more awareness to the company/brand which will hopefully bring more business to the company.


Audience

The main audience for the site is potential customers who most likely own a home or property and are looking for destruction/clean up services, also looking to support local small businesses.

The site will have a simple design and interaction interface as to not scare away customers who are taking a leap in trusting a small business.

I will also use this website to show future potential employers that I am capable of coding a functional website


Goals

The main goal of this website is to get the Construction Dummies name out into the webpage world, it will attract new customers which will increase sales.

This website will also have links to the small social media sites to attract more followers to grow the brand even more.

The website will also have images of before and after photos of jobs that have been completed to serve as a sort of gallery to showcase the good work that is done.
