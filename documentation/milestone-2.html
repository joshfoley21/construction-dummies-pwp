Content Strategy


In header

The header will include a small company logo in the middle with some of the companies values on either side of the logo. There will also be 4 links that will be converted to a dropdown menu on mobile that will link to the about us, the gallery, the services, and the contact us section. The webpage will be one long page so when they click any of the links it will auto scroll them to that section.


In Body

The body will include a picture of both the employees next to a block of text explaining the background to the company (an about us section), below this will have a list of the many services that the businesses provides. Under that will be a few before and after photos of their construction/deconstruction projects, another photo can be added to show the equipment that the company has access to.


In Footer

The footer will include the contact us portion of the website, it will include an email and the phone numbers of the employees, it will also include social media icons that when clicked will bring you to the companies profile on that given social media site.
